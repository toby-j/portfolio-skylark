About Skylark Telemarketing

I'm Wendy Johnson, founder of Skylark Telemarketing.

After working as a telemarketer for a large insurance company, I wanted to provide freelance telemarketing services for the B2B sector.

My aim was to offer a more personal and targeted approach which was fresh, professional and reliable.

I use my experience, personality and a certain amount of style to present your company in a way that is both sincere and informative. I make calls to your target base with enough knowledge of your business to enable an informed discussion and to establish where the needs are.

I can also identify trends within your market sector which help you make better business decisions.

My service has a strong focus on relationship-building to ensure you can be firmly placed in front of your prospects at the optimum time.
